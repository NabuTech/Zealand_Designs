For Employers

Employer Accreditation

Employers must obtain accreditation to hire migrant workers on employer assisted work visas. This is the first step in a three-stage process where Immigration New Zealand assess the employer, the labour market, and the worker.

Learn More >

Employee Visas

Applying for an employee visa in New Zealand involves meeting specific criteria and providing documentation. Our licensed immigration adviser assists by ensuring your application is accurate and complete, and by advocating on your behalf if issues arise.

Learn More >

Job Check

A Job check application is required after employer accreditation to ensure the job meets New Zealand's standards and that no suitable New Zealanders are available. Employers must provide detailed job information and may need to advertise the role before approval.

Learn More >

Legal Advice on Immigration

Legal advice on immigration involves navigating complex regulations and ensuring compliance with all requirements. Our licensed immigration adviser provides expert guidance, helping you understand your options and prepare a strong application.
